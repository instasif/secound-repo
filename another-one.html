Not much is known about the naming of Chandpur, but there are different opinions about it. During the reign of the Bar Bhuiyans, the Chandpur region was occupied by Chandra, the zamindar of Bikrampur. He established a center of governance in the region. According to historian JM Sengupta, the region was named Chandpur after Chandra. On the other hand, the name of this region is Chandpur after Chand Fakir of Purindpur mahalla of Chandpur town (Koralia). According to some, an administrator named Shah Ahmed Chand came here from Delhi in the fifteenth century and established a river port. Chandpur is named after him. My 1st time B-baria tour!!

Author: Asif Hossain

Brahmanbaria is a city in the Chittagong Division of Bangladesh . It is the headquarters of Brahmanbaria Sadar Upazila and Brahmanbaria District . It is the second largest city in eastern Bangladesh after Comilla . Brahmanbaria is one of the oldest municipalities in Bangladesh and was established in 189. Brahmanbaria was declared a district in 1984 . As of 2020, the city of Brahmanbaria has a population of 2,7,736. The town was a part of the greater Comilla district till 1984 . It is the 16th largest city in Bangladesh.
